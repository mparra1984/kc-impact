About

Welcome to KC IMPACT!

KC IMPACT  is a grass roots basketball program dedicated to competitive youth basketball. We are also a Christian, faith based organization that is reaching out to surrounding parishes to help us grow within their communities and surrounding communities. We will be having fun as well as challenging young players to understand the game quicker, explore their basketball talents, and improve their individual skills which will provide an opportunity for them to advance in basketball. We will do this by building their spirits and supporting and assisting them with our knowledge of basketball as coaches and parents.

"FAITH IS USELESS UNLESS PUT INTO MOTION"

FAQs

What is KC IMPACT?

• We are an organization reaching out to parishes in the metropolitan area to help us grow within the community.
• We recruit players and allocate them to teams.
• We ask parishes for gym times to hold practices, scrimmages, leagues and tournaments.

What age does KC IMPACT begin?

• We develop basketball players as young as Kindergarten and plan on working the program throughout their high school experience.

What does KC IMPACT program consist of?

• During the developmental years K-7, we use a universal play book with an easy to understand structure.
• Play development starts as early as 1st grade.
• Older players 8-12 will have more advanced structure concentrating on point production and allowing the athlete to identify their individual and    unique talents in the game of basketball.
• The older athletes are constantly encouraged to become model students and work as hard in the classroom as on the court.
• We follow tournament structures that expose them to college coaches and high levels of competitive play.

Where does KC IMPACT practice?

• Locations vary, simply stated we need gyms.
• We are asking all the surrounding parishes to consider our program and to help KC IMPACT grow within their own community.
• We reach out to surrounding parishes asking if we can rent their gyms, once gyms are established we build teams and develop practice   schedules out of available gym times.

What is KC IMPACT'S operational cost? What do the churches and/or parishes get back?

• We have quite a bit of operating cost. (Gym rentals, uniforms, product apparel, practice equipment, web development, etc…)
• Once the above costs have been met we give all other profit to charity and the church.
• We will have a 60% (church/charity) to 40%(KC IMPACT operational cost) for team development, league play, and tournament play.

What are KC Impact's fundraising programs

• We will sell KC Impact t-shirts which will be used towards fundraising.
• We have and will provide the "GOLDEN TOTE". (more information on this fundraiser will be available soon!)

When does KC IMPACT play?

• We try to and work around kids school structured programs to allow them to play during their school basketball season.
• Typically these programs run in the winter, then KC IMPACT will start up during spring, summer and fall taking breaks as needed within our   sessions.
